LA-Zuberia MALDIVES

Facilities & Services

For sporting and recreational fun, LA-Zuberia Thani Pattaya provides a remarkable range of facilities, including two swimming pools and two private beaches where a wide selection of water sports are available. As a complete resort, the new DFit Fitness Center showcases a fully equipped gymnasium, aerobics room, cardio-theatre room, steam room and sauna. Additionally, there are three tennis courts, and arrangements can be made for golf at any of the nearby 18-hole courses.
